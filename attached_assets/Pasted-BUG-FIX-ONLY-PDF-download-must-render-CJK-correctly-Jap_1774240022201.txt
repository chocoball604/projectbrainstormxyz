- Simplified Chinese (简体中文): 汉语、欢迎、测试
- Traditional Chinese (繁體中文): 漢語、歡迎、測試
- Japanese (日本語): こんにちは世界、自転車

Constraints:
- Fix only PDF generation. Do NOT change QA/telemetry/grounding/export/study flows.
- Do NOT reset/delete DB.

   artifacts/brainstorm/fonts/

   - CJK_JP (collection_font_number = face for Japanese)
   - CJK_SC (collection_font_number = face for Simplified Chinese)
   - CJK_TC (collection_font_number = face for Traditional Chinese)
