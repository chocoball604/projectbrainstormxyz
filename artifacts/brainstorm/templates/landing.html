Project Brainstorm 集思計劃 (測試版)

用於探索階段決策的 AI 原生市場研究

集思計畫是一個實驗性的 AI 原生市場研究平台，旨在幫助團隊在投入資源之前，探索市場與關鍵決策情境。

此版本 -- V1A -- 聚焦於一個核心問題： 在引入任何產品或方案偏見之前，一個市場區隔中「有哪些人存在」，以及這些人可能如何思考或回應一個想法或概念？

傳統市場研究很難有效回答這個問題。依賴真人受訪者的問卷、消費者面板與焦點團體，往往過於緩慢、成本高昂，且在方法上存在結構性偏誤 -- 在亞太地區尤其如此。碎片化的市場、多樣的語言，以及高度依賴文化脈絡的細微差異，使得可靠洞察更困難，也更關鍵。

通用型 AI 工具雖然速度快，但其運作不透明、缺乏在地脈絡，難以建立信任。

集思計畫正是為了探索一種不同的方法而誕生。

此版本的功能

集思計畫V1A支援探索階段研究，可執行民意調查（Survey）、深度訪談（IDI）與焦點小組（Focus Group）。產出形式看似熟悉 -- 研究發現、訪談逐字稿、結構化洞察報告 -- 但其生成方式並非來自真人受訪，而是透過 AI 原生的市場模擬。

平台核心是合成角色（Synthetic Personas，亦可視為模擬消費者的人口代表）：在統計上合理、具有真實感的市場人口代表。這些合成角色以高品質的人口資料、區域脈絡與在地新聞來源為基礎，使研究能呈現具民族誌真實感的反應，而無需依賴即時招募的受訪者。

所有輸出結果皆可被解釋，並附有來源引用，同時經過嚴謹的品質保證流程審查。信心程度會清楚標示，不確定性與限制也會明確揭示。

此版本刻意不處理的問題

此版本不支援概念導向或類別導向的抽樣方式，也不旨在回答以下問題：

「誰會購買這個產品？」

「他們願意付多少錢？」

「哪個品牌會勝出？」

這些問題將留待後續版本探索。

V1A 著重於理解人口結構與反應模式，揭示關鍵張力與行為趨勢，而非預測最終解法或市場結果。

此版本的市場重點

目前的測試版本聚焦於亞太地區的部分主要市場。

隨著平台的持續演進，市場涵蓋範圍將逐步擴展，並同步提升人口真實性、文化基礎與研究方法的嚴謹程度。

為什麼要註冊參與

集思計畫目前仍處於測試版階段。我們正在尋找願意實際使用、挑戰假設，並協助塑造平台未來方向的實務使用者。

如果你從事策略、行銷、洞察、創新、投資或公共政策相關工作，或正在亞太地區創業，我們誠摯邀請你探索本平台並分享你的回饋。

Project Brainstorm (Beta)

AI-Native Market Research for Decision-Making

Project Brainstorm is an experimental platform for AI‑native market research, designed to help teams explore markets and decision contexts before committing resources.

This beta release -- V1A -- is focused on one core question: Who exists in a market segment, and how might those people think or react to an idea or concept -- before any product or solution bias is introduced?

Traditional market research struggles to answer this well. Surveys, panels, and focus groups built on human respondents are slow, expensive, and structurally biased -- especially in Asia‑Pacific, where markets are fragmented, languages are diverse, and cultural nuance matters most.

Generic AI tools move faster, but they are opaque, ungrounded, and difficult to trust.

Project Brainstorm exists to explore a different approach.

What this beta does

Project Brainstorm V1A enables discovery‑stage research using synthetic surveys, in‑depth interviews, and focus groups. Outputs look familiar -- research findings, transcripts, structured insight reports -- but they are generated through AI-native market simulation, not human panels.

At the center of the platform are synthetic personas: lifelike, statistically plausible representations of people in a specific market context. These personas are grounded in high‑quality demographic data, regional context, and local journalism, enabling ethnographic realism without relying on live respondents.

Every output is explainable, source‑cited, and reviewed through a disciplined QA process. Confidence is labeled. Limits and uncertainty are explicit.

What this beta deliberately does not do

This release does not support concept-conditioned or category-primed sampling. It is not designed to answer: “who will buy this product”, “how much are they willing to pay”, or “which brand will win”.

Those questions belong in later versions.

V1A is about understanding populations, surfacing patterns, tensions, and reactions – not predicting solutions.

Where this beta is focused

The current beta is focused on select major markets across Asia-Pacific.

Coverage will expand over time, alongside ongoing improvements to population realism, cultural grounding, and methodological rigour as the platform evolves.

Why sign up

Project Brainstorm is in active beta. We are looking for practitioners who want to try it, challenge it, and help shape what comes next.

If you work in strategy, marketing, insights, innovation, investment, policy -- or are building a business in Asia-Pacific -- we invite you to explore the platform and share your feedback.
