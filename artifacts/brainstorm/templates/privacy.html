Privacy Policy

1. Overview

Project Brainstorm respects your privacy and is committed to handling personal information responsibly.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

We collect only information necessary to operate the Service, including:

Account metadata:

  • Login timestamps
  • Verification and approval status

We do not knowingly collect sensitive personal data.

3. How We Use Information

We use your information to:

We do not sell personal data.

4. AI and Third‑Party Services

  • Project Brainstorm uses third‑party infrastructure and AI services.
  • We do not control how third‑party AI models are trained

You may request account deletion by contacting us.

6. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect user data but cannot guarantee absolute security.

Use of the Service is at your own risk.

7. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the Service constitutes acceptance of changes.

8. Contact

For privacy questions, contact: admin@projectbrainstorm.xyz
